## Executive Summary

The PropTech sector is highly competitive and segmented, with companies specializing in various aspects of the property lifecycle. Houlnd Realty's zero-brokerage, verified marketplace model positions it uniquely against established players by combining cost savings with trust-based lead monetization.

## Key Insights

### What Works in the Market

1. **Zero-brokerage messaging resonates** - NoBroker's success validates the model
2. **Verification builds trust** - Users willing to pay for verified listings
3. **AI is becoming table stakes** - All major players investing in AI features
4. **Video content increases engagement** - 3x higher interest for listings with videos

### What Houlnd Does Differently

1. **Monetizes at point of connection** - Not at listing or via ads
2. **Aligns seller-platform incentives** - Commission only on successful sale
3. **Price transparency as core feature** - Sq.ft pricing enables comparison
4. **Quality over quantity** - Verified listings only, no broker postings
