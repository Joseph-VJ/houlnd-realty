# Houlnd Realty - Project Documentation

> **Zero-Brokerage, Verified Real Estate Marketplace with Precision Price Discovery**

## Overview

Houlnd Realty is a PropTech platform designed to disrupt the traditional real estate market by connecting property promoters (sellers) directly with customers (buyers), eliminating brokerage fees while ensuring trust through verified listings and a unique monetization model.

## Core Vision

- **Zero-Brokerage Model**: Direct promoter-to-customer connections without intermediaries
- **Verified Marketplace**: Strict screening to prevent fake or litigated property listings
- **Precision Price Discovery**: Unique Sq.ft/Price Point filtering system
- **Gated Access Monetization**: Seller contact information protected until customer pays to access

## Target Users

### Customers (Buyers)
- Individuals seeking to purchase residential or commercial properties
- Looking for transparent pricing and verified listings
- Want to avoid high brokerage fees

### Promoters (Sellers)
- Property developers and direct property owners
- Want to list properties without paying upfront fees
- Seek qualified, serious buyer leads

## Key Differentiators

1. **Zero-Broker Policy**: Only direct sellers/promoters can list properties
2. **Gated Contact Access**: Seller phone numbers hidden until customer pays to shortlist
3. **Sq.ft Price Point Filter**: Critical feature enabling price-per-unit comparison
4. **Commission-on-Sale Model**: Free posting with commission agreement upon successful sale
5. **Verified Listings**: Mandatory verification to ensure no fake or litigated properties
